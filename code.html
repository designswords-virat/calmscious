Questions & answers

Everything you might be wondering.

Still unsure? That's completely normal. Reach out and we'll gently answer anything on your mind.

Ask us directly
Do you offer online therapy?
Yes. Dr. Mehar offers both secure online sessions and in-person appointments, so you can choose whatever feels most comfortable and convenient for you.
Is everything I share kept confidential?
Absolutely. Confidentiality is the foundation of therapy — what you share stays private within the ethical and professional boundaries of psychological care.
What concerns can therapy help with?
A wide range, including depression, anxiety, phobias, OCD, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, relationship difficulties and everyday stress.
How do I book my first session?
Simply fill in the enquiry form below or call directly. We'll get back to you to understand your needs and arrange a time that suits you.
What happens in the first session?
The first session is an unhurried conversation. Dr. Mehar listens to understand what you're going through, answers your questions, and together you shape a way forward.
